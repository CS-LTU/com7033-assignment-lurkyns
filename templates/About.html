About the App

Welcome to the Stroke Predictor app! This application is designed to assist users in predicting the likelihood of strokes based on health-related data. It offers features like data uploads, secure patient management, and a user-friendly interface to make health monitoring easier and more accessible.

This project was developed by Osazeme Lurkyns Arthur, a student at Leeds Trinity University, as part of her coursework. If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out via email at 2123782@leedstrinity.ac.uk.
